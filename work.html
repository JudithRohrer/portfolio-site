MeetUp app

This MeetUp app enables users to see upcoming events based on their current location. The location is automatically updated when the app is opened. Moreover, users are able to search for events in other cities and get a quick overview of the number of events being held within the next seven days. Charts visualize how many spots are left for each occasion. In this test-driven development process, I worked with React, Jest, Enzyme, Puppeteer, Cucumber, JavaScript, AWS Lambda, and Recharts.
